Ranking of pizza restaurants in Columbia, Missouri

Columbia is home to a plethora of pizza places, each with their own following of fans who claim their favorite store is the best in town. It’s a hotly debated topic among Columbia natives, transplants and students. This list is my official ranking of pizza places in Columbia, Mo.

1. Pizza Tree

909 Cherry Street
Columbia, MO
65201
(573) 874-9925

Pizza Tree Website

Pizza Tree is a pickup window in downtown Columbia that not only does the classics right, but has some of the most interesting specialty pies in town.

2. Gumbys

1201 E. Broadway
Columbia, MO
65201
(573) 874-8629

Gumbys Website

Gumbys is a pizza chain popular among students in downtown Columbia, with great deals and loaded pizzas.

3. Shakespeares

225 S 9th Street
Columbia, MO
65201
(573) 449-2454

Shakes Website

Shakespeares is Columbias most popular pizza place, with multiple locations around town. The flagship downtown location is celebrating 50 years of business this year.

4. Wise Guys Pizza

7 N 6th Street
Columbia, MO
65201
(573) 443-7777

Wise Guys Website

Wise Guys Pizza in the back of McNallys Irish Pub is a hidden gem, and a great spot for a late night pizza in downtown Columbia.

5. Tonys Pizza Palace

17 N 5th Street
Columbia, MO
65201
(573) 442-31889

Tonys Pizza Palace Website

Tonys has been in downtown Columbia for over 40 years, and is a local favorite.

6. Pappos Pizza

10 W Nifong
Columbia, MO
65203
(573) 818-1790

Pappos Website

Pappos is a regional sit-down pizza restaurant chain, with a full bar and a family-friendly atmosphere.

7. Imos Pizza

601 Business Loop 70 West
Suite 213D
Columbia, MO
65203
(573) 886-7373

Imos Website

Imos is a St. Louis original, known for its provel cheese blend, notably not recognized as cheese by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration.
